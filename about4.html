CAPTAIN OF BLACKBEARD'S PIRATES

Ice Age (アイスエイジ, Aisu Eiji): Kuzan places his hand on the ground, and instantly freezes every/any thing in sight into a completely frozen wasteland and anyone or thing within the vicinity into completely frozen ice statues.

The actual extent of the ice can spreads for miles and last for over a week. The literal kanji reading is "Hyouga Jidai", a Japanese four-kanji term for the ice age.
